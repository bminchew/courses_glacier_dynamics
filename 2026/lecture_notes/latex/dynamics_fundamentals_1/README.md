# Ice Dynamics I: Strain Rates, Simple Shear, and Ice Sheet Profiles

Lecture notes introducing the strain-rate tensor, Glen's flow law (stated), the parallel-slab velocity profile, the shallow-ice approximation, the Vialov ice sheet profile, and the perfectly plastic limit. Comparison with observed ice sheet profiles.

## Files

- `dynamics_fundamentals_1.tex` — Lecture notes (LaTeX source)
- `dynamics_fundamentals_1.pdf` — Compiled lecture notes
- `dynamics_fundamentals_1_figures.ipynb` — Jupyter notebook generating all lecture figures
- `dynamics_fundamentals_1_exercises.ipynb` — Student exercises
- `dynamics_fundamentals_1_exercises_solutions.ipynb` — Exercise solutions (excluded from git)
- `figures/` — Generated figures (PNG and PDF)
