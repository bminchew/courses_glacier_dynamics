# Glacier Mass Balance Processes

Lecture notes covering mass balance definitions, accumulation and ablation processes, spatial and temporal patterns, snow-to-ice densification, balance velocity, the vertically integrated continuity equation, submergence and emergence velocities, and the height-mass balance feedback.

## Files

- `mass_balance.tex` — Lecture notes (LaTeX source)
- `mass_balance.pdf` — Compiled lecture notes
- `mass_balance.ipynb` — Jupyter notebook generating all lecture figures
- `figures/` — Generated figures (PNG and PDF)
